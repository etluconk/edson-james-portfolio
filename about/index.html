About Me

I've been growing up in Seattle, Washington for most of my life. I'm passionate about the things I make and I love sharing those creative outlets with the people I love. I care deeply about music, visual art and interactive media. Currently, I'm primarily pursuing piano, cartooning, and video game development.


More recently, I've also taken up a huge interest in fashion and other means of personal expression. I'm fascinated by the ways people can be advocative and aware of their own wellbeing throughout their lives, especially during childhood.

I'm also interested in religion and politics. This is because of my experiences and relationships with people of varying beliefs, but also because of the ways I've noticed social media and news outlets shaping (and polarizing) so many peoples' opinions. While I might talk about these topics on this website, I won't ever be talking about my personal beliefs here. In my opinion, my political and religious views aren't really relevant to the kinds of creative projects I'm showing here :)

Anywho...
